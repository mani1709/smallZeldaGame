In this game, the goal is to beat the final boss!
Controls:
	Up: Player goes up
	Down: Player goes down
	Right: Player goes right
	Left: Player goes left
	B: use bombs (after aquired)
	Space: use sword
	Enter near Doors: opens certain doors where a key is needed
	Enter near Pots: hold pot
	Enter with pot held: throw pot
	M: Minimap


Starts when dragging the folder to the Love2d.exe
